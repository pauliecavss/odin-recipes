Pauls Pasta

Description

The recipe is half something I found online and half of what i thought would be a better combination to perfect the pasta.

Ingredients

    Pasta
    Grilled/Fried Chicken
    1/2 Cup Buffalo Sauce
    1/2 Cup Blue Cheese
    1 TBSP Cream Cheese
    1/2 Stick of Butter
    Cajun Seasoning

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, cajun and garlic powder
  2. Cook pasta and grill chicken on stove with olive oil
  3. Put half stick of butter in a pan and mix in 1/2 cup of blue cheese, 1/2 cup of buffalo sauce, and 1 TBSP of cream cheese
  4. < cut up chicken with sauce and pasta
  5. ENJOY!!
